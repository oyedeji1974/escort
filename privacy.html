Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

At Omnivo, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our massage marketplace services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a high-quality ad-posting experience, we may collect:

  • Account Data: Your name, email address, and contact details when you register.
  • Ad Content: Any photos, descriptions, or contact links you include in your massage service advertisements.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our site to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to:

  • Facilitate the posting and management of your ads.
  • Connect clients with professional massage therapists.
  • Send important updates regarding your account or site changes.
  • Maintain a safe and secure community for all users.

3. Public Information

Important: When you post an ad on Omnivo, the information you provide in the ad (such as your business name, location, and phone number) will be visible to the public. Please do not share sensitive personal information that you do not want others to see.

4. Data Protection

Omnivo employs industry-standard security measures to protect your data. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal private information to third-party marketers.
